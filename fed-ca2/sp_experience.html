Courses added in SoC
DCITP
Diploma in Common ICT Programme

DCITP is a semester long programme designed to give students a broad introduction to the Infocomm landscape and help them gain insight into the occupations and job roles, career pathways and employment opportunities in the various Infocomm sectors.


DCITP will also help students lay a strong foundation in programming and IT fundamentals.


After the end of semester 1, students will get to choose one of the three IT courses (DAAA, DISM, DIT).



Click here to find out more.

DAAA
Diploma in Applied AI & Analytics

DAAA is for students who are interested in developing AI applications that can benefit our world.


Students can gain a competitive edge pursuing their passion as a data analyst, data engineer or an AI practitioner.


They can also face new challenges and projects closely related to solving real-world problems.



Click here to find out more.

DISM
Diploma in Infocomm Security & Management

DISM is for students who are interested in defending against cyber attacks.


Students can gain a competitive edge for a boost in their future studies and career.


They can also look forward to an interesting curriculum that covers offensive attacks, defensive methods and investigative skills.



Click here to find out more.

DIT
Diploma in Information Technology

Information Technology (IT) is the driving force behind many digital transformations we see and use today, including the Smart Nation vision.


In Singapore, there continues to be a strong demand for IT professionals in Software Development and User Experience (UX) design, and in Immersive Simulation Technologies such as Augmented and Virtual Reality.


DIT is positioned to focus on Software & Applications development with three specialist tracks (Software Development, UX design, Immersive Simulation)
